By accessing or using LumoChat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Text inputs, prompts, and messages submitted during AI conversations
  • Text used for image generation requests
  • Audio input when you use voice-to-text features
  • Communications with us, such as emails sent to our support team

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use LumoChat, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system, and app version)

  • Approximate location information (derived from IP address, if applicable)

2. Use of Audio Recording Permission (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO)

LumoChat requests the android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO permission solely to enable the speech-to-text (voice recognition) functionality within the app.

Specifically:

  • The microphone is accessed only when you actively choose to use voice input.
  • Audio data is used exclusively to convert spoken words into text.
  • We do not record, store, or monitor audio in the background.
  • Audio is not used for advertising, profiling, or unrelated purposes.

If you do not grant this permission, you may continue to use LumoChat with text-based input, but voice input features will be unavailable.
