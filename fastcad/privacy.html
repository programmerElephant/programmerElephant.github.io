Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

Welcome to FastDWG - CAD Viewer ("FastDWG", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

FastDWG is a CAD drawing viewer designed for engineers, architects, construction teams, field reviewers, students, and other professionals who need to open, inspect, measure, convert, and review DWG and DXF drawings on mobile devices.

By using FastDWG, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Files and Content You Choose to Use

FastDWG may process files and content that you choose to open, import, review, convert, or share through the app, including:

  • DWG and DXF drawing files
  • Exported or converted files, such as PDF, SVG, and image files
  • Recent file information, project organization data, file names, and related metadata
  • Drawing review notes, measurement results, layer selections, and viewing preferences
  • Text prompts, questions, drawing descriptions, or other inputs submitted to AI assistant features

Where possible, drawing files and related project data are handled locally on your device. Some features, such as cloud-based AI assistance, file conversion, analytics, crash reporting, or support requests, may require data to be processed by service providers.

1.2 Information You Provide to Us

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Messages, emails, or attachments you send to our support team
  • Feedback, bug reports, feature requests, and related communications
  • Information included in AI assistant conversations or engineering review requests

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use FastDWG, we or our service providers may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

  • Device model, operating system, language, region, and app version
  • Usage information, such as feature interactions, timestamps, and performance data
  • Crash logs, diagnostic data, and error reports
  • Approximate location derived from IP address, where applicable

2. Device Permissions and Engineering Tools

FastDWG may request device permissions only when needed to provide specific app features. The permissions available to you may depend on your device, operating system, and app version.

  • Files and storage: Used to open CAD drawings, manage recent files, save converted files, and share exports.
  • Microphone: Used when you actively use the decibel tool or any audio-based measurement feature. We do not record, store, or monitor audio in the background.
  • Motion, orientation, or sensor data: Used for tools such as the level tool and other field reference functions.
  • Location, altitude, or environmental data: Used only if you enable features that require site reference information, such as altitude-related tools.

You can manage app permissions in your device settings. If you deny a permission, the related feature may be unavailable, but other parts of FastDWG may continue to work.

3. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide CAD viewing, layer review, measurement, conversion, and sharing features
  • To maintain recent files, project workflow organization, and user preferences
  • To operate AI engineering assistant roles for architecture, construction, cost, mechanical drawings, standards, and drawing review
  • To improve app performance, reliability, usability, and compatibility with CAD files
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and respond to support requests
  • To protect the Service, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations

4. AI Assistant Features

FastDWG may include AI engineering assistant features that help users ask questions, review drawing-related information, or receive general engineering workflow assistance. Information you submit to AI features may be processed by third-party AI service providers to generate responses.

Do not submit confidential, sensitive, regulated, or proprietary project information to AI features unless you have the right to do so and accept the associated processing. AI responses may be incomplete or inaccurate and should not be treated as professional engineering, architectural, legal, safety, or compliance advice.
