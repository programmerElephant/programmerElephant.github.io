Privacy Policy

Vibra - Music Player

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vibra - Music Player ("Vibra", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using Vibra, you agree to the practices described below.

Vibra is designed as a music player. Your music files, playlists, playback history, and library information are primarily processed on your device to provide the app's core features.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Vibra, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Local music and media information: song titles, artist names, album names, artwork, playlists, folders, and playback preferences stored on or selected from your device.
  • App settings and preferences: playback mode, equalizer settings, theme choices, language settings, and other configuration choices you make in the app.
  • Device and usage information: device model, operating system version, app version, performance data, crash reports, and basic diagnostic events used to maintain and improve the app.
  • Contact information: your email address and message content if you contact us for support or feedback.

We do not require you to create an account to use Vibra. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government ID numbers, precise financial information, health information, or biometric identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide music playback, library management, playlist features, and app personalization.
  • To remember your settings and improve the reliability and performance of Vibra.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, protect app security, and develop new features.
  • To respond to support requests, questions, and feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights when necessary.

3. Permissions

Vibra may request access to your device's media library, storage, notifications, or other features depending on your device and the functions you choose to use. These permissions are used only to provide the requested app features, such as finding and playing music files or showing playback controls.

You can manage app permissions through your device settings. Some features may not work properly if required permissions are disabled.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only in the following situations:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors may process diagnostic, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or support information on our behalf.
  • Legal and safety reasons: we may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Vibra, or others.
  • Business transfers: information may be transferred if Vibra is involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.

5. Third-Party Services

Vibra may use third-party tools for analytics, crash reporting, advertising, app distribution, or payment processing. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy settings and policies provided by your app store, device platform, and any third-party services connected to the app.

6. Data Retention

Local music library data and app preferences generally remain on your device unless you delete them, reset the app, or uninstall Vibra. Support emails and diagnostic records are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To make a privacy request, contact us at support@bytrabbit.com. We may need to verify your request before taking action.

8. Children's Privacy

Vibra is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Use

If you use Vibra from outside the country where our service providers operate, your information may be processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your location.
